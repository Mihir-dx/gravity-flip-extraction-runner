Gravity-Flip Extraction Runner

A high-stakes, fast-paced 2D arcade runner that strips away traditional infinite-survival tropes and replaces them with an intense, psychological risk vs. reward extraction loop.
Instead of running until you inevitably fail, you must choose whether to extract safely to secure your hard-earned loot, or dodge the portal to push your Risk Tier multiplier further while risking a complete wipeout.

Action, Keyboard Input, Mouse / Screen Tap Input
Invert Gravity, Spacebar, Click/Tap anywhere on Canvas
Navigate Menus, "Space (Start), S (Shop), C (Credits)",Click on Visual Screen Buttons

Core Gameplay Systems
1. The High-Stakes Extraction Loop
  - Binary Movement: The player cannot jump; they instantly snap between the ceiling and floor, balancing momentum across mid-air dead zones.
  - The Extraction Choice: Every 1,000 points, an Extraction Portal sweeps across the screen. Crashing into it secures your current run's crystals permanently. Skipping it multiplies your crystal rewards and game speed, but dying afterwards means losing everything collected during that run.

2. Persistent Meta-Progression
   All extracted crystals are committed to the browser's persistent localStorage. Players can spend their permanent bank in the Quantum Upgrades Shop on three tactical power-ups:
  - Magnetic Pull (Utility): Expands your item collection radius to pull in crystals safely.
  - Kinetic Shield (Survival): A single-use shield that shatters on impact, granting a 1-second invulnerability window to save you from a fatal mistake.
  - Gravity Dampener (Skill-Cap): Triggers a brief 150ms micro slow-motion frame upon flipping gravity to help you weave through ultra-dense hazard fields.
